While most people agree that things like proportionality and competitiveness are important democractic ideals, 
we rarely agree on how to measure these things.

<!--more-->
Here are some resources on building metrics of electoral fairness.
- [Measuring partisan fairness](/publications/political-geometry/02-BernsteinWalch.pdf) explores different ways to measure how fair maps are with respect to parties.
- Read a bit about how fairness is (or isn't) baked into the design of U.S. democracy in ["Political Geometry."](/publications/political-geometry/03-Editors.pdf)
