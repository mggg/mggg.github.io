We analyze geography, demographics, and voting patterns in Santa Clara, CA in order to compare the likely performance of various systems of electing members of the City Council. We find that either citywide at-large transferable voting or a custom transferable
voting plan as the best remedy for the alleged CVRA violations in Santa Clara City Council elections.
