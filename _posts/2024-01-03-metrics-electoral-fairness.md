While most people agree that things like proportionality and competitiveness are important democractic ideals, 
we rarely agree on how to measure these things.

<!--more-->
Here are some resources on building metrics of electoral fairness.
- [Chapter 2](/publications/political-geometry/02-BernsteinWalch.pdf) of [Political Geometry](/gerrybook)  explores different ways to measure how fair maps are with respect to parties.
- [Chapter 3](/publications/political-geometry/03-Editors.pdf) discusses how fairness is (or isn't) baked into the design of U.S. democracy.
